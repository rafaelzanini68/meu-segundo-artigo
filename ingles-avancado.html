1. High-Level Phrasal Verbs

  • To call off: Cancelar algo.
    "The mission was called off due to heavy rain."
  • To put out: Extinguir (fogo).
    "The team took hours to put out the wildfire."
  • To pull over: Encostar o carro.
    "The driver was told to pull over for inspection."

2. Third Conditional (Hypothetical Past)

Estrutura essencial para relatórios e provas militares:

If + Had + Done, would have + Done

"If the reinforcements **had arrived** sooner, the situation **would have been** controlled."

3. Essential Vocabulary

Hazardous: Perigoso
Casualty: Vítima
First Aid: Socorros
Enforcement: Aplicação (da lei)
